Hotel/Travel Information

CGO 2015 will be held at a hotel in close proximity to the San Francisco International Airport (SFO), at the San Francisco Airport Marriott Waterfront Hotel, which is a 20 minute Taxi or Uber ride from downtown San Francisco.

The Marriott is sold out at the CGO rate on Tuesday night. All other nights are available at the conference rate.

Click here to get the special rate ($179/night) at the Conference Venue. In-room Internet is free with complimentary Marriott Rewards registration. This rate may not be available after January 16, 2015.

Directions:

By BART: Take the BART to the SFO stop. Then take free Hotel shuttle from airport.

By Airplane: Fly to SFO. Take the free Hotel shuttle.

By Car: 1800 Old Bayshore Highway, Burlingame California.
