Registration Details

Conference registration includes admission to the conference, digital conference proceedings, reception and excursion, breakfast, coffee breaks, and lunches. Additional workshop and tutorial registration also includes coffee breaks and lunches.

During the registration process you can purchase 1 or 2 days of workshop/tutorial passes, as well as extra social event tickets. Workshop/tutorial only registrations are also available on the main registration page. Authors can purchase an additional page beyond the standard 11-page paper length.

Any registered attendee of CGO is welcome to attend the PPoPP and HPCA conferences.

Students attending U.S. universities are also eligible to apply for CGOâ€™s NSF funded student travel grant, and all students who are authors of a paper are eligible to apply for a SIGPLAN travel grant. Note that funds received from travel grants cannot be used to pay conference registration fees as the student fees are already discounted.
